Sulfuric Acid
Sulfuric acid is one of chemical material that corrosive. Sulfuric acid is an mineral acid with sulfur, oxygen, and hydrogen element. It's colorless and viscous. Also, it dissolve with water in all concentration. H2SO4 its chemical formula.

Introduction to Sulfuric Acid

Sulfuric acid in pure form is so corrosive to other materials. It removes water from any substance when in contact. Sulfuric acid is hygroscopic and easy to absorb vapor from air. Much heat will be released when this substance mixed with water. Because its corrosive nature, when our skin contact with sulfuric acid, this thing will be dangerous for our body. Its acid characteristic will burns our skin. So need special handling with this acid material. Sulfuric acid is one of material that useful for chemical industry,so it's an importance comodity. This substance produced with some methode, like contact process, wet process, "led room" procees, and some other process.

The Physical Properties of Sulfuric Acid

  • Its molecular weight or molar weight is 98,079 gr/mol
  • Density 1,84 gr/cm3
  • Its boiling point is 337oC
  • Melting point is 10oC
  • Anhydrat sulfuric acid is a polar liquid
  • Its has high electric conductivity

The Chemical Properties of Sulfuric Acid

  • Sulfuric acid easy to react with most of base and produce the corresponding sulfate. For example, reacting oxyside copper (II) and sulfuric acid :

    CuO(s) + H2SH4(q) → CuSH4(q) + H2O(l)

  • Sulfuric acid can substitute acid that weaker than its salt on reaction with acetate natrium.

  • Aqueous sulfuric acid react with many metal through single-moving reaction like other acid, produce gas and hydrogen salt.

    Fe + H2SH4 → H2 + FeSH4

Utilization of Sulfuric Acid

  • Useful for fertilizier manufacture
  • Metal and steel production
  • To produce phosphate acid
  • Useful for chemical industry
  • For oil and gas distillation
  • Useful as cleaner to remove rust on the metal and steel
  • Act as cathalyst to change cyclohexanone into caprolaktam that used for produce nylon
